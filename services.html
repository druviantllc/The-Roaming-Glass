Our Services Menu

Signature Cocktails

Our expert mixologists create custom cocktails tailored to your event’s theme and preferences. From seasonal ingredients to unique recipes, every drink is crafted with precision. You provide the alcohol, and we handle the rest, ensuring each drink is an experience in itself.

Event Packages

Our event packages are designed to cover everything from bartending services to equipment and glassware. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or private party, we offer packages that cater to every event size, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable bar experience. Note: Alcohol must be provided by the customer.

Customization Options & Add-Ons

Customized Glassware

Enhance your event with personalized glassware that adds a touch of elegance and individuality to your bar setup. Your guests will be impressed by the attention to detail.

Customized Drink Menu Design

Let us design a beautiful custom drink menu that aligns with your event’s theme. This service includes everything from design to presentation, ensuring your bar menu is as memorable as the drinks themselves.

Themed Garnishes

Elevate your cocktails with our themed garnishes, providing an extra touch of creativity and sophistication. Perfect for themed events, weddings, and special occasions.

Our mocktail station offers a variety of non-alcoholic options that are just as delicious and sophisticated as their alcoholic counterparts. Ideal for guests who prefer not to drink alcohol.

Champagne Service

Our champagne service is perfect for special occasions, offering a dedicated station where guests can enjoy a sparkling toast to celebrate your event in style.
